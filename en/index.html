Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2026

DomGrp ("we", "us") provides this Privacy Policy ("Policy") describing how user information is handled in the mobile game "Cubelines" (the "App"). Please read this Policy before using the App.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not collect information that directly identifies you, such as your name, postal address, phone number, or email address. We may, however, collect and use the following:

  • Nickname: an optional display name you enter when submitting to the leaderboard.
  • Score and gameplay progress: used to provide the leaderboard and game features.
  • Anonymous user ID: a random ID generated by Firebase Authentication (anonymous sign-in). It does not identify you personally.
  • Advertising-related information: for ad delivery, including advertising identifiers (Android Advertising ID / iOS IDFA, etc.), device and OS information, approximate location, IP address, and ad impression/interaction data.
  • Anti-abuse information: tokens used by App Check to verify that requests come from a genuine instance of the App.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide App features such as the online leaderboard
  • To display, deliver, and optimize advertising
  • To prevent fraud and unauthorized use
  • To improve quality and fix issues

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services. Information is processed in accordance with each provider's terms.

  • Google AdMob (advertising) — How Google uses data in ads / Google Privacy Policy
  • Firebase (Authentication / Cloud Firestore / App Check) by Google LLC — Firebase Privacy and Security / Google Privacy Policy

Through these services, information may be transmitted to and stored on servers outside your country (e.g., the United States).

4. Advertising and Tracking

  • The App displays advertising via Google AdMob (banner, interstitial, and rewarded).
  • Advertising identifiers may be used to personalize ads.
  • iOS: In accordance with App Tracking Transparency, the App presents a prompt requesting permission to track. You may decline; the App remains fully playable (ads may be non-personalized).
  • You can reset your advertising identifier or opt out of personalized ads in your device settings (Android: Settings > Google > Ads / iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking).

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not primarily directed to children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information, please contact us using the details below.

6. Data Storage and Deletion

Nicknames and scores are stored on a server (Cloud Firestore) to provide the leaderboard. To request deletion of your record, please contact us below. In-app save data is stored on your device and is removed when you delete the App.

7. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information we collect against leakage, alteration, and unauthorized access.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy as needed. Significant changes will be announced on this page. The updated Policy takes effect when posted here.
